A Stained-Glass Star, Shattered

  • they're not ready
  • to lose it all just yet,
  • but when has that
  • ever stopped fate?

Theirs is a tragedy, but not for lack of trying. The sky was playful, the earth was content, and the ocean taught itself to dance along the horizon and the shoreline, the chorus of the tides in rhythm.

A man walked over one day and ripped up the earth without heed for its screams. He shattered the sky with the horizon, and when the ocean surged in fury, he poisoned it in the abyss.
